Spaghetti with meatballs

Description

Spaghetti and meatballs is an Italian-American dish consisting of spaghetti, tomato sauce and meatballs. Originally inspired by similar dishes from southern Italy, the modern version of spaghetti and meatballs was developed by Italian immigrants in the USA

Ingredients:

  • 400g of spaghetti
  • 500g of ground meat (half beef, half pork)
  • 1/2 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cans of 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot with boiling salted water, following the package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the ground meat, breadcrumbs, milk, egg, Parmesan cheese, parsley, garlic, salt, and pepper. Knead well and form medium-sized meatballs.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the meatballs and cook, turning them occasionally, until they are golden brown on all sides. Remove the meatballs from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and cook over medium heat until tender and translucent.
  5. Add the crushed tomatoes, sugar, bay leaf, and dried oregano. Cook over medium-low heat for 20-25 minutes, until the sauce has thickened.
  6. Return the meatballs to the skillet with the sauce, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until the meatballs are fully cooked.
  7. Drain the spaghetti and serve it on plates. Top with the meatball sauce and sprinkle with additional grated Parmesan cheese and fresh chopped parsley if desired.
